Biometrics

Camera Types

IP Cameras

Digitise and process video within the camera itself, transmitting data over an Ethernet network. Supports higher resolutions (4K+), smarter motion detection, and easier scalability.

Analogue Cameras

Send raw video signals over coaxial cable to a DVR for processing. A cost-effective and reliable solution for standard-resolution surveillance requirements.
